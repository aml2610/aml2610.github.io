About me

Who am I?

I am a Second Year Computer Science student at The University of Birmingham. I'm passionate about programming, technology and everything computational. As I see it, the best developers are the ones who never stop learning, philosophy I've adopted as well.

What do I do?

I am a Junior Software Engineer at ClickTravel, where I usually program in Java. In my free time, I take a great interest in Web Design, Web Development and Android Programming. You can find out more by taking a look at my CV, Github repos, or Linkedin profile.

Web Design & Dev

Android Dev
